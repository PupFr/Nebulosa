Privacy Policy - Nebulosa Bot

Effective Date: July 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Nebulosa Bot ("we," "our," or "us") provides intelligent Zoom meeting management services through our Telegram bot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Telegram user ID and username
  • Zoom account information (when you authorize our app)
  • Meeting participation data (camera status, audio status, participant names)
  • Chat messages sent to the bot

2.2 Zoom Meeting Data

  • Meeting participant lists and status
  • Meeting duration and attendance records
  • Chat monitoring for moderation purposes
  • Host and cohost management activities

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide automated meeting moderation services
  • Monitor meeting compliance and participant behavior
  • Enable multipin access management
  • Send notifications and alerts to designated channels
  • Maintain meeting logs for administrative purposes
  • Improve our bot functionality and user experience

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only:

  • With meeting hosts and cohosts for moderation purposes
  • In designated Telegram channels for monitoring activities
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted transmission of all data
  • Secure storage of OAuth tokens
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Meeting logs: 30 days
  • User activity data: 90 days
  • OAuth tokens: Until you revoke access
  • Account information: Until you request deletion

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke Zoom authorization at any time
  • Opt-out of data collection by stopping bot usage

8. Third-Party Services

Our bot integrates with:

  • Zoom: Subject to Zoom's Privacy Policy
  • Telegram: Subject to Telegram's Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
